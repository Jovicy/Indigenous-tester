Careers

Join our mission to empower indigenous Lagosians and foster sustainable growth across Lagos. At IILASG, we believe that passionate, dedicated individuals can make a lasting difference. Explore our career opportunities and be part of our journey toward positive change.

Why Work with Us?

When you join IILASG, you become part of a vibrant, mission-driven organization that values innovation, diversity, and collaboration. We provide opportunities for growth, meaningful work, and a chance to contribute directly to empowering indigenous communities.

Our Benefits:

  • Opportunities for professional development
  • Inclusive and collaborative work environment
  • Contribution to impactful projects across education, business, culture, and political advocacy
  • Networking opportunities with industry experts and community leaders
