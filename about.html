About Us

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a thriving, inclusive society where indigenous Lagosians can fully participate in the city's growth and development. We envision a future where our people have access to quality education, sustainable employment, and opportunities for cultural expression and political representation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower indigenous Lagosians by fostering economic growth, promoting cultural preservation, and advocating for political inclusion. We are dedicated to providing access to education, creating sustainable employment opportunities, and nurturing leadership development. Through collaborative efforts and impactful initiatives, we aim to build a stronger, more inclusive Lagos where indigenous voices are heard, valued, and uplifted.

Our People

We are a collective of indigenous Lagosians—entrepreneurs, cultural advocates, youth leaders, and professionals—working together to create positive change. With the collective strength of our diverse membership, we aim to achieve our vision for a better Lagos.

Meet Our Management Team

IILASG is governed by a dedicated board of leaders and experts in diverse fields, including business, culture, education, and social development. Our board is committed to guiding the organization towards achieving its mission and ensuring our programs make a lasting impact on the indigenous community: management team, advisory team, political affairs coordinators, LGA coordinators.
