Our Programs

IILASG offers a variety of programs aimed at supporting the growth of indigenous Lagosians, including:

Entrepreneurship and Business Development

Empowering indigenous Lagosians with resources and mentorship to build thriving businesses and contribute to the local economy.

Cultural Preservation and Promotion

Celebrating and preserving the rich cultural heritage of Lagos while promoting tourism and indigenous arts.

Youth Empowerment and Leadership Training

Equipping young Lagosians with leadership skills, educational opportunities, and pathways for a brighter future.

Political Engagement and Advocacy

Encouraging active participation in governance and advocating for the political rights of indigenous communities.

Our Key Projects

Trade and Investment

Through this project, we facilitate partnerships, trade initiatives, and investment opportunities to boost local businesses and entrepreneurs, contributing to the economic development of Lagos

Culture and Tourism

We actively promote the rich culture, traditions, and heritage of indigenous Lagosians by creating tourism opportunities that showcase our unique identity to the world.

Youth Rebranding

This initiative aims to inspire the next generation of Lagosians by focusing on education, skills development, and career pathways. We are committed to creating more opportunities for youth engagement and empowerment.

Political Appointments and Rights

We advocate for fair political representation and encourage indigenous Lagosians to take active roles in the political processes, ensuring their voices are heard and their rights are protected.

E-Commerce

We are building a platform ( Csdil E-commerce ) where indigenous products, arts, crafts, and services can be marketed to a global audience. This e-commerce initiative seeks to create sustainable revenue streams for our members while promoting indigenous talent and entrepreneurship.
